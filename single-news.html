Sling Health

Sling Health is an organization that aims to empower and support aspiring entrepreneurs in the field of healthcare innovation. It operates as a student-led biotechnology incubator, providing a platform for students to develop and launch their own healthcare solutions. It originated as a program at Washington University in St. Louis. Sling Health offers mentorship, resources, and collaborative environment to help students transform their ideas into tangible products or services that address pressing healthcare challenges. One of Sling Health’s annual event, Sling Health Demo Day, serves as a innovation demonstrated by the participating student teams. During Demo Day, teams present their projects and present their business plans to industry professionals, investors, and fellow students. It provides an opportunity for teams to receive valuable feedback, gain exposure, and potentially secure funding or partnerships to further develop their ventures. Niura compeited in the Sling Health Demo Day Competition and was one of the the top finalist among all other amazing startups.

Sling Health is a healthcare innovation organization that originated in Washington University in St. Louis in 2013. The organization provides resources, training, and mentorship to foster the development of entrepreneurship in students who aim to tackle clinical problems through novel solutions. They accomplish these goals by teaching the skills necessary for invention and implementation of new technology, prototyping, testing, protecting intellectual property, etc. Demo Day, Sling Health’s annual flagship event, serves as a platform for students to showcase their progress and pitch their ideas to entrepreneurs, clinicians, and investors, allowing them to build the early network for future success. They also provide financial prizes that can be used to jump start and help transition the project into full-fledged companies.
